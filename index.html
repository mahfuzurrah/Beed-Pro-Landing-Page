About Us

BEED.PRO Procurement AI-Assistant has been founded by Nordic Procurement AB Consulting Company, Finance, Operations, Transformation and Information Technology Senior Managers, leveraging on their accumulated experience for many decades in the corporate and government procurement, operations management, transformation and automation, as well as adopting current technological advancements of artificial intelligence. Using Generative Pre-Trained Transformer (GPT) technology, fine-tuned for procurement operations with continuous learning and improving capabilities based on self-aggregated data, the Procurement AI-assistant gives confidence to buyer companies that their procurement decisions are accelerated, price quotations are automated and the comparisons of limitless proposals are unbiased. The Procurement AI-Assistant also accelerates contract management cycle and supplier onboarding, not letting you to miss any approaching deadline and gives tools for re-negotiating the best procurement deal with no hassle.

Product Overview

No matter you are registering as a buyer or supplier all public information about the company will be auto-uploaded to simplify the registration process.  

  • Add new company to your Supplier Contact List and the company information will be auto uploaded.

  • Stay updated for the changes of the contact persons details automatically.

  • Upload procurement contracts with one click and let AI-Assistant to extract all intelligent data about payment terms, notice period and other details, and get automatic notifications of the contract status change.

  • Renew the terms via automated price quotation process.

  • Procurement AI-Assistant will generate the document of requests for Proposals (RFP) by precisely defining the product and service descriptions matching to the needs of the specific employee category.

  • Start asking for price quotation among pre-filtered and pre-validated suppliers matching to your procurement category in 3 simple steps. No emails! No phone calls! All in one place!

  • Receive limitless offers and let AI-Assistant to compare them with just one click to get valuable insights and comments for each pair of proposals, even if the proposals structures are different.

  • Look at the best price quoted by your competitors and adjust your proposal in real time.

  • No limitations, you are free to offer any price, betting on the proposed product or service quality.

  • Be the first to learn what is needed for your customers and make the best offer in one click.
